#4 Giraffes


Giraffes


Here are some fun facts about Giraffes:


  • Giraffes are the tallest mammals on earth

  • Giraffes can run as fast as 35 mph for short distances and can for longer distances at 10 mph

  • A giraffe's neck is too short to reach the ground

  • Giraffes only need to drink water every few days
