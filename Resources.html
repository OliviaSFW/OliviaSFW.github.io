How to learn German

There are many apps like Duolingo that you can get that can guide you while you are learning


Another good way to learn is to watch one of your favorite shows in German by changing the language. Make sure that you know some basics before doing this.

Reading books is another good way to learn, even if you dont understand anything. There are many books that offer in German like the Harry Potter series.

Watching German media can also be helpful if you have access to it.
